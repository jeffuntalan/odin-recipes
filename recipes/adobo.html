Adobo

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds chicken thighs
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup vinegar
  • 1 head garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon peppercorns
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 cup water

Instructions

  1. Combine all ingredients in a pot.
  2. Marinate for 30 minutes.
  3. Simmer over medium heat for 30 minutes.
  4. Remove chicken from the pot.
  5. Heat oil in a pan.
  6. Place chicken in the pan.
  7. Broil until browned.
  8. Serve with rice.
