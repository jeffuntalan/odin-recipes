Sundubu Jjigae

Ingredients

  • 1/2 package of soft tofu
  • 1/4 cup of kimchi
  • 1/4 cup of pork
  • 1/4 cup of clams
  • 1/4 cup of enoki mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup of green onions
  • 1/4 cup of onion
  • 1/4 cup of zucchini
  • 1/4 cup of water
  • 1/2 tablespoon of minced garlic
  • 1/2 tablespoon of soy sauce
  • 1/2 tablespoon of red pepper powder
  • 1/2 tablespoon of sesame oil
  • 1/2 tablespoon of salt
  • 1/2 tablespoon of sugar
  • 1 egg

Instructions

  1. Prepare the ingredients: cut the tofu, kimchi, pork, clams, enoki mushrooms, green onions, onion, and zucchini into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Heat a pot on medium heat and add the minced garlic.
  3. Add the pork and stir-fry until it's no longer pink.
  4. Add the kimchi and stir-fry for 1 minute.
  5. Add the clams and stir-fry for 1 minute.
  6. Add the onion, zucchini, and enoki mushrooms and stir-fry for 1 minute.
  7. Add the water and bring it to a boil.
  8. Add the tofu, soy sauce, red pepper powder, salt, and sugar.
  9. Let it boil for 5 minutes.
  10. Crack an egg into the pot and let it cook for 1 minute.
  11. Add the green onions and sesame oil.
  12. Remove from heat and serve hot.
